Franchising Opportunities

For over 25 years, Sushi Tei has provided diners with an exquisite Japanese dining experience. The company’s development within Asia continues to grow steadily with over 75 outlets in 8 countries. We continue to seek new global markets for international expansion.

The company’s reputation is second-to-none and we offer a friendly and family-oriented dining environment along with the finest Japanese cuisine. All our outlets are strategically located at prime areas of Singapore with wallet friendly prices.

We are looking for individuals or organizations who are interested in the possibility of securing a master franchise within their chosen territory. Our ideal partner should have food and beverage experience, extensive market knowledge and have the financial position to fully develop the Sushi Tei Brand within their own market.

We also have a team of dedicated staff who will provide training, business and operations support for our franchisees and address any potential concerns or problems that arise.

Passion, personality and a robust culture are what we look for in our partners. If you feel that you fit the description above – We would love to hear from you!

Our franchise department is based in Singapore. Please write us an introductory email at franchise@sushitei.com and we will get back to you.

We thank you for your interest and we look forward to making Sushi Tei a truly international brand together.
